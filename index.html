Welcome To My Founders & Coders Page

Burhan Baalwaan London, UK Software Developer Learner

How it all started

It all started with a game called Human Resource Machine. This is a game that mimics the elements of programming. But at the beginning, I had no idea that there was anything more to it than it being an enjoyable game. I would break down problems in the game when stuck on a challenge, debug and go through each line to identify mistakes. I started researching the concept and idea behind the game and in simple terms came to realise that it was a programming game.

Journey to code

Further research into programming lead me to CodeAcademy where I took a short HTML and Javascript course. At that point my interest and curiosity for coding only grew. Then I came across Founders & Coders.

Though I had been sceptical about attending meetups and putting myself in a vulnerable position as a newbie to coding, it became apparent to me after the first meetup, that being in an environment with like minded people was so much more fun than learning solo. The Founders & Coders meetups put heavy emphasis on collaborative learning, which I really believe helped me in my development.

Opportunities

Eventhough, I did somehow stumble across coding, I realised through Founders & Coders that it brings people together and offers many great opportunities educationally and socially. There is a lack of awareness and diversity within tech. It would be amazing to see more and more people get involved in the progression of tech.

Motivation

I now realised that coding has not only become a passion of mine but has also developed my thinking process. I have always been driven by my curiosity, which is what lead me into loving to code.

I hope to gain relevant skills from my experience at Founders & Coders which will not only be a setting stone to a career in tech, but also allow me to give back in a meaningful way to the community.

When you learn, teach. When you get, give.
